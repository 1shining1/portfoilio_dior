DIOR JEWELRY

Victoire de Castellane. The Artistic Director of Dior Jewelry is revolutionising the traditional codes of jewellery with her dream-like, detailed decoration. Her richly coloured narrative creations are inspired by themes dear to Monsieur Dior and tell extraordinary stories.

HIGH JEWELRY

DIOR DIOR DIOR

The high jewellery collection Dior Dior Dior unveils treasures of virtuosity inspired by haute couture and the many kinds of lace present in Christian Dior’s archives. Cutting bits of lace, placing them on the hand and transforming them into colourful jewels was the original idea behind this collection. Then twisting the fabric’s weave and cutting out its arabesques and intertwinings so that the lace can unfurl its curves on necklaces, rings, bracelets and earrings.
