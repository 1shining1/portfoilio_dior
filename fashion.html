SET DESIGN
With this new collection, Maria Grazia Chiuri begins a new chapter devoted to the arts and feminism. It is contemporary dance celebrated as a universal language and liberating act, an art of movement. Greeting the guests, a monumental, immaculate structure standing in the heart of the racecourse displayed several quotes: “dance, dance, otherwise we are lost”, “dance is the movement of the universe concentrated in an individual”, “everything must come from the heart, must be lived”, “nothing is more revealing than movement, the body says what words cannot”, “I am not interested in how people move; I’m interested in what makes them move” and “the story comes from inside the body”.
